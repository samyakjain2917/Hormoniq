Mood-Based Food Suggestions

Healthy Food Swaps Tailored to You

Based on your mood and PCOS type, Hormoniq suggests balanced, nourishing food swaps to help you feel your best every day.

Mood: Feeling Low

  • Swap sugary snacks with dark chocolate + walnuts
  • Choose sweet potatoes instead of white rice for serotonin boost
  • Drink herbal teas (like chamomile or ashwagandha)

Mood: Anxious or Stressed

  • Replace coffee with matcha or tulsi tea
  • Go for avocado toast instead of processed breakfast bars
  • Snack on almonds and pumpkin seeds

Mood: Bloated or Sluggish

  • Choose cucumber + mint-infused water over soda
  • Opt for steamed veggies instead of fried foods
  • Snack on pineapple or papaya to aid digestion

Mood: Craving Comfort Food

  • Swap creamy pasta with chickpea pasta and avocado sauce
  • Have banana + peanut butter instead of cookies
  • Try homemade soups with lentils and herbs

PCOS Type: Insulin-Resistant

  • Swap white bread with sprouted grain bread
  • Use cauliflower rice instead of white rice
  • Drink apple cider vinegar in water before meals

PCOS Type: Inflammatory

  • Choose berries over processed sugary treats
  • Replace dairy with almond or oat milk
  • Opt for turmeric-laced golden milk in the evening
